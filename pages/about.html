Our Story

Founded in 2019 by Monique Richards (formerly Monique Barbier), our foundation emerged from a serendipitous encounter that would change many lives forever.

After completing her university studies and participating in an exchange program in the U.S., Monique embarked on a journey as a tour guide with MyGuide website, offering her services free of charge. It was during one of these tours that she encountered a tourist visiting a slum area where women were crafting beautiful paper beads despite lacking basic resources.

Inspired by the resilience of these women, this chance meeting sparked a movement. What began with sourcing buckets from Poland grew into a comprehensive initiative supporting education and economic empowerment.

Today, we proudly sponsor the education of 100 children and empower women through sustainable craft enterprises, helping them renovate their homes and secure better futures for their families.

Our Programs & Initiatives

Education

Sponsoring children's education and ensuring access to quality learning opportunities through comprehensive support and mentorship.

Economic Empowerment

Assisting women's groups in marketing their unique crafts, fostering self-reliance and financial independence through sustainable business practices.

Community Support

Launching initiatives to identify and support children unable to attend school while providing additional community resources and development programs.
